About Student Management System

The Student Management System is designed to help schools and institutions efficiently manage student records. Add new students, view detailed records, and track student activities through a modern and professional dashboard.

Dashboard

Quick overview of student records and activities. Monitor performance and summaries at a glance.

Add Student

Easily add new student information and keep all records organized professionally.

Records Table

View, sort, edit, and delete student data efficiently in a clean and modern table.
